Hi, I’m Yixin

I help beginner and lower-intermediate Chinese learners understand Chinese more clearly and use it more naturally in real life.

My lessons are not just about memorizing phrases. We will work on your actual learning problems, such as sentence building, speaking confidence, grammar clarity, pronunciation, and real-life expressions.

My Teaching Philosophy

I don’t believe one single method works for every Chinese learner. Some students need more comprehensible input. Some need clear grammar explanations. Some need speaking practice and corrections.

That’s why my lessons are personalized. I usually combine clear explanations, real-life examples, guided speaking practice, and personalized corrections.
