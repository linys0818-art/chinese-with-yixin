Personalized Chinese Lessons

This course is not a fixed template. Before we begin, I will first understand your current Chinese level, your goals, and the difficulties you are facing right now.

Based on your situation, I will design a learning plan that fits you. The lessons may include listening and speaking practice, clear explanations, real-life Chinese expressions, review materials, homework, and after-class support.

My goal is not to make you memorize random phrases, but to help you build a clearer path and keep moving forward.

What Can You Get from Yixin’s Private Lessons?

Activate Your Chinese Listening and Speaking

In class, I will use as much Chinese as you can comfortably understand and gradually increase the amount of Chinese input. You will also be encouraged to express your own ideas in Chinese, so your speaking ability can grow through real use.

A Clear Learning Path

Language learning needs effort and practice, but clear guidance makes everything easier. Each lesson has a clear goal, topic-based practice, structured output, and review materials, so you won’t feel lost.

FAQ

How long is one course cycle?

One cycle lasts 4 weeks. Each week includes at least one 50-minute live lesson. I recommend a steady and consistent learning rhythm.

How many cycles do I need to reach my goal?

It depends on your current level and your goal. We need to talk first before I can give a realistic suggestion. In general, reaching a meaningful goal usually takes at least 4 cycles. Language learning is a long-term process, and big changes are hard to achieve in a very short time.

Can complete beginners join?

Yes. Complete beginners are welcome. I can help you build a strong foundation and learn to form Chinese sentences with a Chinese way of thinking, instead of creating confusing sentence structures from the beginning.

Can short-term learners join?

It depends. If you only want to try a few random lessons without a clear goal or learning intention, this course may not be the best fit.

But if you have a clear short-term goal, such as traveling to China in six months or preparing for an HSK exam in three months, and your current level matches the goal after evaluation, you are welcome to contact me.

Can I get a refund if the lesson pace is not suitable for me?

Yes. After the first lesson, if you feel the course is not a good fit, I can refund the remaining lessons in the current cycle. Starting from the second lesson, the remaining lessons in that cycle are non-refundable.

What is included in the course service?

In general, the service includes a personalized learning plan, weekly live lessons, after-class review materials, and support for questions during the learning process.
